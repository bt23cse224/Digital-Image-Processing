SHANNON-FANO CODING
-Count how often each character appears.
-Sort characters by frequency (descending).
-Keep splitting the list into two parts so their total frequencies are as close as possible.
-Assign '0' to one part and '1' to the another.

HUFFMAN CODING
-Count character frequencies.
-Build a binary tree by combining the two least frequent characters repeatedly.
-Traverse the tree: left = '0', right = '1'.
